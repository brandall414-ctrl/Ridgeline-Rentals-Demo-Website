        Peace of Mind

        Optional Gear Protection

        Rentals include a refundable security deposit hold. If you'd rather skip the hold and climb worry-free, add our Gear Protection option at checkout. It covers accidental damage from falls and normal use of the course. Doesn't cover negligence or unreturned gear.

        Without Gear Protection, a refundable hold is placed on your card at rental: $150 per kit or $60 per lanyard. Maximum hold per transaction is $250. Holds are released on the same day you return your gear. Multiple kits or lanyards? Gear Protection stacks — $16 for two kits, $10 for two lanyards, $15 for three lanyards, and so on.
